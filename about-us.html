About DigitalIndians.org

Connecting India's businesses to the digital world. We help businesses grow by providing a platform to showcase their products and services to millions of potential customers.

Our Story

DigitalIndians was founded in 2025 with a simple mission: to help Indian businesses succeed in the digital era. We recognized that many small and medium-sized businesses across India had extraordinary products and services but lacked the digital presence needed to thrive in today's market.

What started as a small directory of local businesses has grown into a comprehensive platform connecting millions of consumers with businesses across the country. Today, DigitalIndians is India's fastest-growing business discovery service with over 4.6 crore businesses listed and 19.8 crore monthly users.

Our team is composed of passionate individuals who believe in the power of digital transformation to empower businesses of all sizes. We're constantly innovating and improving our platform to provide businesses with the tools they need to reach more customers and grow their operations.

Our Mission & Values

Our Mission

To empower every business in India with digital tools and visibility, enabling them to reach their full potential in the digital economy.

Our Vision

To create India's most trusted and comprehensive business discovery platform that connects consumers with relevant businesses quickly and efficiently.

Our Values

We believe in integrity, innovation, inclusivity, and impact. These core values guide every decision we make and every feature we develop.
